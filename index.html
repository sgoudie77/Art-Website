About

Jackie Doucette was raised on Newfoundland’s rugged and wild west coast. She is inspired by nature’s offerings as well as by the colourful and vivacious culture that is Newfoundland.

You can find Jackie exploring hidden beaches and trails along the top coast of the island in search of treasure and adventure. She creates custom art work for gifts for that special occasion, contact her for more details.
